Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about working with me

What services do you offer?

I specialize in full-stack web development, mobile app development, and custom software solutions. This includes responsive web design, React and React Native applications, backend API development with Spring Boot, database design, and deployment to production environments.

What is your development process?

I follow an agile development approach with regular communication and iterative delivery. The process typically includes initial consultation, requirement analysis, design mockups, development sprints, testing phases, and deployment. You'll receive regular updates and have opportunities for feedback throughout the project.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a full-stack web application typically requires 6-12 weeks. Mobile apps generally take 8-16 weeks depending on features. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your application stays up-to-date, secure, and running smoothly. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements as needed.

What technologies do you work with?

My primary tech stack includes React and React Native for frontend/mobile, Spring Boot and Node.js for backend, PostgreSQL and MongoDB for databases, and various cloud services for deployment. I'm always learning new technologies to provide the best solutions for each project's unique needs.

How can I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply reach out through LinkedIn, GitHub, or any of my social channels. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project goals, requirements, and timeline. From there, I'll provide a detailed proposal and we can begin bringing your vision to life.
